Currency

All of the enhancemnts players can aquire in Helldivers 2 are very beneficial, only if you have them of course. Players will need to obtain various currency in order to purchase said enhancements. Fortunately, there is no need to purchase any of these in-game currencies with real money, as all of them are able to be found throughout the game.

All Currency

Here you'll find all in-game currency, their usage, and how to obtain them

All Currency
Name Usage How to Obtain
Requisition Slips Purchase Stratagems Obtained based on mission performance; can be found randomly around the map
Super Credits Purchase Armor that appears daily Found randomly around the map
Medals Purchase various items in the Warbond Battle Pass Obtained based on mission completion; can be found randomly around the map
Samples (Common, Rare, Super) Purchase Ship Module upgrades Found randomly around the map; must extract with Samples to obtain

Currency to Prioritize

Players should focus on obtaining Samples the most. This is because Samples aren't too hard to find, but they can be easily lost upon death. Requisition Slips and Medals are guarenteed so long as players complete their missions, and Super Credit items are usually not that special compared to what can be bought with the other currencies.
