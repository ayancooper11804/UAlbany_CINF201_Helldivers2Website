Armor

Players can equip various armor to enhance their gameplay. Armor comes with stats known as Armor Rating, Speed, and Stamina Regen. Thes stats affect how much damage players take, their movement speed, and how fast players regain their stamina respectively. Armor also comes with a Passive Ability, which is something extra that can further improve players' stats and items in game. Such abilities include increasing Stim capacity (an item that allows players to heal from damage), increasing grenade capacity, making players less visible to enemies, reduce damage taken, and more.

Armor Types

Here you'll find information about Armor Types

Armor Types
Type Brief Explanation
Light Faster movement and stamina regen; low protection
Medium Balanced protection, movement speed, and stamina regen
Heavy Slow movement and stamina regen, high protection

Passive Abilities

These Passive Abilities are listed from best to worst

  1. Med Kit: Increases Stim capacity by 2 and increases their duration by two seconds.
  2. Democracy Protects: 50% less chance of getting killed by lethal damage and prevents bleeding from chest hemorrhages.
  3. Scout: Markers placed on maps scan the area every two seconds, reduces enemy detection by 30%.
  4. Fortified: Reduces recoil while crouching or laying down by 30% while granting 50% resistance to explosions.
  5. Engineering Kit: Reduces recoil while crouching or laying down by 30% and increases grenade capacity by 2
  6. Servo-Assisted: Increases grenade throwing range by 30% and adds 50% bonus to limb health
