Ship Modules

At higher difficulties, it can become harder to survive to the point where not even Stratagems are guarenteed to save players. However, Stratagems can be improved through upgrades of the Ship Module. These upgrades affect various traits of Stratagems, such as reducing Stratagem cooldown time, increasing damage, maximizing ammo, and more. These upgrades can only be purchased via the samples players collect in missions, so players are advised to collect and extract with as many samples as possible.

Ship Module Upgrades

Here you'll find the upgrades provided when fully upgrading a Ship Module

Ship Module Upgrades
Category Upgrades
Patriotic Administration Center Reduces cooldown of Support Weapon and Backpack Stratagems by 10%; Support Weapons deployed with max ammo
Orbital Cannons Orbital Stratagems have increased damage past center explosion and cooldowns are reduced by 10%; Orbital Barrage Stratagems fire one additional time
Hangar Eagle Stratagems: Cooldown reduced by 10%, provides a rearm with a cooldown of 10%, and can be used one additional time per rearm
Bridge Mini-map vision range is increased by 50 meters; Eagle Stratagems rearm cooldown reduced by 10%; better control of Hellpod (device used when entering mission or respawning)
Engineering Bay Sentry, Emplacement, and Resupply Stratagems' cooldowns are reduced by 10 percent; Sentry Stratagems' health counts are increased by 50 percent; Emplacement stratagems are deployed instantly
Robotics Workshop Sentry Stratagems: Deployment time reduced, ammo increased by 50%, and rotate speed increased

Upgrades to Prioritize

There are tons of Ship Module upgrades to purchase, so there are a few players recommend to prioritze right away to help have a better experience. Players recommend uprgading the Patriotic Administration Center, Orbital Cannons, and Hangar categories to the first level. This ensures Support Weapons are deployed wth max ammo, Orbital Stratagems do extra damage, and Eagle Stratagems have a reduced cooldown time respectively.
